Solution Validation (Phase 1: Technical Spike - 4 weeks)
Calendar intelligence automation (not just booking logistics):
[ ] Conflict detection accuracy: Can we achieve <1 error per 100 bookings across complex calendar rules?
[ ] Multi-party coordination: Does overlapping availability finder work with 3-5+ participants (depositions, mediations)?
[ ] Data capture for Phase 2: Can we extract pattern signals (time preferences, meeting type patterns, relationship frequency) from Phase 1 usage?
[ ] Integration reliability: Do Google/Outlook Calendar APIs provide sufficient data and sync speed (<30 sec booking-to-calendar-event)?
Category Validation (Phase 1: Competitive Audit)
New category, not feature competition:
[ ] White space confirmed: No one positions as "Calendar Intelligence for Professional Services" with billable hour attribution
[ ] Category gap: Existing tools are "Scheduling Automation" (Calendly) OR "Practice Management" (Clio), not calendar intelligence
[ ] Newly possible: Is LLM-based Phase 2-3 intelligence truly the enabler that makes strategic calendar understanding feasible?
[ ] Framing acceptance: Do customers buy "calendar intelligence with billable hour ROI" vs. "expensive Calendly"?
[ ] Defensibility: Clear moat through data accumulation (calendar patterns + case outcomes + strategic context)?
