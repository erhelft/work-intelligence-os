### **Flowtrace**

- **Company name:** Flowtrace
- **Website URL:** https://flowtrace.co
- **Tagline:** "Create insights of company calendars and online meetings so you can steer your meeting culture with management metrics"
- **What the product is:** Meeting analytics and transformation platform for organizational time governance and meeting culture optimization.
- **Main value proposition:** Flowtrace analyzes corporate calendars and meetings to create actionable management metrics about meeting overload, inefficiencies, and productivity patterns. It provides leadership visibility into meeting costs, team time allocation, and benchmarks against industry best practices while delivering AI-powered recommendations for meeting culture improvement through organizer nudges.
- **Target customer:** B2B; Mid-market to large enterprises (50-1000+ employees); Leadership, managers, founders, HR teams; Organizations focused on productivity and meeting culture transformation.
- **Positioning:** "Steer your meeting culture with management metrics." "Reduce meeting overload. Increase productivity. Make better decisions. Reveal meeting costs. Benchmark teams."
- **Primary use case:** Calendar auditing, meeting cost analysis, team time allocation measurement, zombie meeting detection, policy enforcement through behavioral nudges, and cultural transformation of meeting practices.
- **Source:** https://flowtrace.co
- **Focus:** Both visibility/analytics and enforcement (behavioral nudges and meeting policy guidance).
