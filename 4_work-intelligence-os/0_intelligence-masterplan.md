### Phase 2: Business Use Case Framework & Selection
**Status**: ✅ Complete
**Dates**: October-November 2025

**Documents Created**:
- `3_framework-business-usecase.md`: Mapped 10 Primitives of Business Intelligence across 6 dimensions of business understanding. Established 27-dimension evaluation framework (Learning Value + Business Viability + Practical Feasibility). Core insight: 98/2 rule—strategic understanding emerges from synthesizing operational signals.
- `3_selection-business-usecase.md`: Evaluated all 10 primitives. Top 3: Time & Calendar (74/90), Projects (70/90), Meetings (64/90). Calendar identified as optimal first product.

**Main Outcomes**:
- Calendar Management scored highest: perfect data access, universal scarcity, immediate value, low friction
- Calendar acts as integration hub (proximity 9/10) connecting to meetings, projects, communication
- Practical feasibility is primary differentiator—most primitives fail on access/adoption despite strong learning/business scores
- Domain-specific work product (Primitive 10) critical but vertical-dependent

### Phase 3: Vertical Selection Framework & Selection
**Status**: ✅ Complete
**Dates**: November 2025

**Documents Created**:
- `4_framework-vertical.md`: Established 11 criteria across 3 categories (Big Enough, Suitable for Learning, Approachable). Mapped 12 vertical clusters. Sweet spot: modular but interconnected work.
- `4_selection-vertical.md`: Evaluated 12 verticals. Professional Services (11/11 strong marks) clear winner. Tech/SaaS strong second (8/11). Financial Services high friction despite good fundamentals.

**Main Outcomes**:
- Professional Services (law firms, consulting, accounting) is optimal vertical—exceptional across all dimensions
- Billable hour model creates perfect business attribution (time saved = dollars earned)
- Tech/SaaS validated as strong alternative or fast-follower vertical
- Cross-org learning potential is hidden moat driver
- Regulation/integration complexity eliminates Financial Services and Healthcare as entry verticals

---

### Phase 4: Combined Use Case × Vertical Selection
**Status**: ✅ Complete
**Dates**: November 2025

**Documents Created**:
- `5_business-usecase-with-vertical.md`: Created 7-parameter combination framework. Evaluated 9 combinations (3 use cases × 3 verticals). Calendar × Professional Services scored 63/70—highest of all combinations.

**Main Outcomes**:
- **Winner identified: Time & Calendar Management × Professional Services (Law Firms)**
- Billable hours create perfect business attribution (partners bill $500-1000/hr)
- Partners manage 25-30+ meetings/week (high volume for learning)
- Premium pricing viable: $75-150/user (3-7x horizontal tools)
- Calendar beats Projects as first product: higher volume, faster feedback, simpler integration
- Clear manual role exists (legal assistants) providing training data and adoption path
